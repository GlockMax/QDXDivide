Status Bar

There are hints on the left and 3 information blocks on the right of the Status Bar.

In the first of them you can see the number of line and the name of a CNC program. This is a program and line pointed by the cursor in the (Work > Mode > Wireframe) mode. Or the program and line that resulted the pointed spot in the (Work > Mode > Result).

In the second block you can see the X, Y and Z-axis displacement and the distance from the basis point. The basis point is defined by the last click in the Graphic window. Thus you can measure the axes displacements and the distance from any point dynamically.

In the third block you can see the pointed or snapped point's coordinates.
