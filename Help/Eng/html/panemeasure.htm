Measurement pane

Measurement pane displays additional information on selected surface of the stock. Measurements can be done after calling main menu Inspect > Measure...

You can show/hide Measurement pane from main menu Settings > Panes.

You can change the pane position on screen, dock it to any other pane, make it autohide.

You can set background color, size and color of text of Geometry pane. This parameters are set in Settings > Parameters... > Design, выбрать ‘онѕанели или “екстѕанели. WTF?

In measurement mode you have just to drag mouse cursor over the surface, so the surface parameters would be displayed in Measurement pane.

Uncut surface of stock - coordinates of the point under the cursor.

Plane - plane normal and coordinates of the point under the cursor.

Cylinder - convex or concave, center coordinates, cylinder axis direction, cylinder radius.

Torus - convex or concave, center coordinates, axis direction, two radiuses of torus.

Sphere - center coordinates and radius.
