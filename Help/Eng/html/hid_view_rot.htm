Rotate XYZ, Rotate X, Rotate Y and Rotate Z Commands (View Menu)

This commands are used for dynamic objects turning.

 Rotate XYZ - dynamic turning around any of the axes. Move mouse pointer to the desired turning center, press and hold left mouse button, move the mouse and the object will be rotating, command will stop working when you will finish holding the mouse button.

Other ways of activating this command:

From the Toolbar:

Ctrl + Left Mouse Button

Hold Middle Mouse Button and press Left Mouse Button.

 

 Rotate X - dynamic turning around the X-axis.

 Rotate Y - dynamic turning around the Y-axis.

 Rotate Z - dynamic turning around the Z-axis.
