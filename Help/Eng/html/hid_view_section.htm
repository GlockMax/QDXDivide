Section

In the Main Menu: Inspect > Section

Quick activating:  icon.

Building the dynamic section of the machined stock. This command is available in the Stock window when the stock does exist. If there is no stock then you will see the alert message:

When you push  icon the section dialog box is opened, you see section parallel to the ZX-axis that goes through the origin of coordinates or "Program Zero".

You can set one of the XY, ZX, YZ or the plane parallel to the screen to be the secant plane. You have to choose it from the list:

You can also define the rotation angle around the axis. The rotation axis name is the first letter in the axis name. For example if the ZX-axis is chosen with the angle of 30 degree then the plane will be rotated around the Z-axis with the angle of 30 degree.

You can change the section's position using the slider. Direction is the normal to the chosen section plane. Put the mouse cursor over the slider and move it to the left and to the right. You will see the sectioned stock on your screen. The number under the slider will be changing showing the real distance from the origin of coordinates.

You can put this number manually to build the section on the defined distance from the origin of coordinates.

You can change the section position on the defined Step. Put the number in millimeters in the Step window (for example: 5). Then push left and right arrow-buttons to get the stock sections on the distance multiple to 5mm.

You can define the secant plane on your screen. Push the button with arrow and show the place where you want secant place to go through.

The section can be filled with some color if Fill check box is checked:

If this check box will be unchecked - section will be transparent and you will be able to look inside the stock.
